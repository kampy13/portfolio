About Me

Kevin Lewis, Founder

Hello! My name is Kevin Lewis and I am a professional freelance web developer and founder of Echo Summit Digital. I really enjoy working with clients to bring their visions to life. I specialize in lightning-fast development and responsive design. I get deep satisfaction from creating sites that are both beautiful and functional. I have worked in a variety of industries including tech, finance, and politics. I decided to start my own business to gain flexibility and control over my work. In my spare time I enjoy climbing, exploring far-flung destinations, and spending time with my family.
